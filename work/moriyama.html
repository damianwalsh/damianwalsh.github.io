Umbraco CMS specialists

Moriyama, an Umbraco Gold Partner specialising in development, hosting, training and support commissioned me to redesign their website in August 2013.

The brief was to develop a new responsive design, providing the company with flexible components enabling Content Editors and Developers to update the website without authoring CSS.

I built front-end templates (HTML/CSS/jQuery) for Moriyama’s developers to integrate with the existing Umbraco CMS and developed a new Campaign Monitor email template. I also updated stationery and promotional items reflecting the new visual design.
