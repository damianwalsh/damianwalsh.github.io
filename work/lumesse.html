World class talent management

Lumesse, a recruitment software provider approached Moriyama in January 2014 with a brief to develop lead generation landing pages and responsive website.

The objective for landing pages was reducing sign-up form abandonment rates while the website refresh project involved developing a prototype demonstrating responsive design applied to key page templates.

My role was developing initial concepts and working in conjunction with a Visual Designer translating corporate guidelines produced by Lumesse's internal brand management team into static front-end templates (HTML/CSS/jQuery) for Moriyama's developers to integrate with the Umbraco CMS, LinkedIn and Salesforce.
