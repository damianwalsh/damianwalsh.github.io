From pregnancy to toddler

I was approached by Moriyama in July 2013 to update the Tesco Loves Baby Club website with a mobile-friendly design.

My role was to develop a prototype demonstrating responsive design to the client utilising existing website content and structure.

The new templates didn't make it into production, nevertheless building the prototype offered a valuable learning experience of retro-fitting responsive design into an existing website.
