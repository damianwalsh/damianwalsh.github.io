<div class="site-main-section">
  <div class="container pad-one-col">
    <p>User-testing sessions were conducted at throughout the project to identify usability issues and ensure concepts met user expectations. Participants completed a series of pre-defined scenarios recorded using <a href="http://silverbackapp.com/">Silverback</a> following think-aloud protocol. Each session captured interactions, task completion rates, usability scores, overall satisfaction ratings, questions and feedback. Findings were reported through executive summary reports illustrating key insights and recommendations.</p>
