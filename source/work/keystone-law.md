    <p class="lede">Commercial law practice Keystone Law approached Moriyama in August 2014 with a request for proposal to redevelop their company website.</p>
    <p>The objective was updating the website with a responsive layout using existing branding, content and structure. My role involved research and delivery of a pitch presentation which communicated our thinking and provided an initial design suggestion.</p>

  <div class="container pad-one-col">
    <p>The proposal demonstrated how a collaborative design process following <a href="http://alistapart.com/article/the-core-model-designing-inside-out-for-better-results">Core Model</a> principles could be undertaken to optimise user experience by matching business and user goals while retaining existing website content and structure.</p>
    <p>The client ultimately decided not to proceed with the recommendations, nevertheless developing the proposal offered me valuable experience in pitching to prospective clients.</p>
